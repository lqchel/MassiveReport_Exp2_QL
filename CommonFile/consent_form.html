Hello! Thank you for your interest in our study.

We are interested to find out your general impression on some images that reflect day-to-day lives. You will see each image briefly (less than half a second), then we will present you a series of small image patches, and ask you whether or not each patch were part of the presented image. After completing trials, we will ask you to complete a short questionnaire.

This experiment will take approximately 40 minutes of your time. Don't worry! Further instructions will be provided before the experiment starts, and you get three practice trials.

All data will be kept confidential. If you have any question at this stage, please contact the investigators above.
